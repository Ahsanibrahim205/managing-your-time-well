Managing your
time well

How this technique can improve your life

Positive benefits

  • Greater sense of control as you manage time, not time managing you
  • Social time appears to last longer
    - on days off
    - at time before/after work
  • Improved psychological feelings of better work/life balance
  • Greater ability to relax and recharge
  • More calm
  • Ability to handle increased tensions as they arise then to ‘switch off’

Reduction in

  • Emotional burn-out
  • Over-concern with time
  • Strong feelings of being pressurised by time
  • Feeling overwhelmed
  • Anger and hostility
  • Helplessness
  • Resignation
  • Diverse psychosomatic ailments
